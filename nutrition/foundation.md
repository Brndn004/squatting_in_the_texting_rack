# Nutrition foundations

## Major points

1. Primary goal: hypertrophy.
2. 5-25% caloric surplus.
3. Center caloric intake around maintenance.
4. Use intra-workout nutrition if workouts last >1 hour.
5. Get meals within 4 hours before / after working out.
6. Fiber later at night.

### On (2)

* I should aim for the higher end, or 25% caloric surplus.

### On (4)

* Half a gram of carb per minute after the first 30 minutes.
* E.g. 5-6 sour peach candies from minute 30 until the end.

### On (6)

* Keeps you fuller.
* Slows digestion.
* E.g. cottage cheese + wheat thins, plus a turkey breast salad.

## Macronutrient breakdown

Goal:
* Protein: 1.2 - 1.6 g per lb of lean body mass.
* Fats: minimum of 20% of caloric intake goal.
* Carbs: whatever is left in your calorie intake goal.

Calories per gram:
* Protein: 4 cal / g
* Fat: 9 cal / g
* Carb: 4 cal / g 

## Supplements

### Tier 1

* Protein powder, focused on leucine content.
* Creatine, 3-5 grams per day.
* Caffeine.

### Tier 2

* Multivitamin.
* Fish oil.
* Preworkout (esp. caffeine).
