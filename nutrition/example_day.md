# Example day

For 3200-3400 calories.

## Peanut butter protein smoothie + vitamin

* 1.5 cup milk
* 2 tbsp peanut butter
* 36 g whey protein
* 1 banana
* 27 g oats
* 5 g creatine
* Multivitamin

## High-protein chicken wrap + coffee

* 4 oz chicken breast
* 42 g brown rice
* 1 cup spinach
* 28 g cheese
* 2 tbsp salsa
* 2 tbsp sour cream
* 45 g (1 large) whole wheat tortilla
* Cup of coffee

## Turkey and veggie stir fry

* 150 g ground turkey
* 1.5 cups frozen veggies (broccolli, peppers, carrots)
* 1 tbsp soy sauce
* 84 g brown rice
* 1 egg

## Apples and peanut butter yogurt dip

* 170 g greek yogurt
* 2 tbsp peanut butter
* 1 tbsp honey
* 185 g apples
* 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
